About the Course

The "English for Dev's" course is designed specifically for developers and tech professionals to improve their English language proficiency in the context of the tech industry. This course covers the following key areas:

  • Technical Vocabulary and Terminology
  • Effective Written Communication
  • Professional Email Writing
  • Presenting Technical Concepts
  • Participating in Meetings and Discussions
  • Job Interview Preparation
  • Networking and Socializing
  • Cross-cultural Communication
  • Language for Collaboration and Teamwork
