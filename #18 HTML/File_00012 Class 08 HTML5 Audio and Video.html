Class 8: HTML5 Audio and Video

  1. Adding Audio with <audio> Element

    2. Audio can be added using the `

    <audio controls> <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> Your browser does not support the audio element. </audio>
  2. Adding Video with <video> Element

    3. Video can be added similarly using the `

    <video controls> <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4"> Your browser does not support the video element. </video>
  3. Video Formats and Codecs

    4. Different browsers may require different video formats and codecs for compatibility.

  4. Controlling Playback and Controls

    5. The `controls` attribute adds playback controls to the audio or video element.

  5. Fallback Content for Non-Supporting Browsers

    6. Provide fallback content between the opening and closing tags for browsers that don't support the element.

    <audio controls> <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> Your browser does not support the audio element. </audio>
